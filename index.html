Who Are We?

Logistat, is the multitenant last-mile delivery platform that offers seamless delivery automation. On-boarded customers have web and mobile applications customized and branded for use. The web App is accessed via a url placed on a browser while the mobile App is downloadable from the ‘Playstore’. LOGISTAT comes with an ADMIN module, SUPER ADMIN, USER module and a RIDER module all performing specific functions, Enjoying various levels of rights and privileges. With permission to talk to a database housing data related and interrelated in various ways. It addresses the un-optimized and traditional way in which business is done in the last-mile delivery process of today by giving it a platform for digital transformation. Logistics plays a huge role within today’s economy. It is estimated that the UK Logistics & Posts Sector is worth 55 billion to the economy and comprises 5% of the UK GDP. The industry also employs 1.7m people.
